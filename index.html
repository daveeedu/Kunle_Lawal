Who is Justice adekunle lawal?

Justice Adekunle Abiodun Ibrahim Lawal known best as Kunle Lawal was born on the 28th of December 1978 to Oluwasegun and Tanimowo Lawal in Zaria, Kaduna state. As a young boy, he was exposed to Christianity and Islam which each parent practised while developing a keen movie/music Heritage. Kunle Lawal is an Alumunus of Ahmadu Bello University Staff school (Bz Annex), Demonstration secondary school and Ahmadu bello University Zaria. He is Married to Fifi and they Blessed with a Son - Demi Omar Lawal.

Entrepreneurial Life - Arriving Abuja in 2002 with a few friends began Roswell Inc, a private company with an interest in IT. He worked with both private and public organisations offering consultancy in information technology till 2014. In 2015, as a private consultant handling logistics for the ASM and the CDC in Washington, he handled logistics in Nigeria. Kunle had a brief stint in Public Service, Serving as P.A (IT and Publicity) to the Minister of Women's Affairs between 2009 - 2011. He went on to start Sandslaw Consulting which he serves as Managing partner till date

Political Life - Apart from becoming the president of the press club in secondary school, his political life remains uneventful until the Occupy Nigeria situation to which Kunle lent his support. Kunle always has demanded more in politics and governance and this led him to set up a private group with some friends - The Orange party which rose him to prominence teaching civic education and noticed by the KOWA party of which he joined and rose to Ag. National Publicity Secretary in 2016. During his time at KOWA party, he worked on party Constitution, developed a five minute registration system within the party, and Head of Delegation of the National Executive Council for the Osun gubernatorial primaries and declared to run for Senate in the Federal Capital Territory.
